FAQ

Common Questions

What are your shipping options?
We offer free standard shipping (5-7 business days) on all orders. Express shipping (2-3 days) is available for $49. White glove delivery and installation services are also available for larger furniture pieces.
What is your return policy?
We want you to love your purchase. If you're not completely satisfied, you can return any item within 60 days for a full refund. Items must be in original condition with all packaging materials. Return shipping is free for defective items.
Can I customize furniture pieces?
Absolutely! We offer customization options for wood finishes, dimensions, and hardware. Our Professional and Enterprise plans include access to our full customization service. Contact our design team to discuss your specific needs.
What warranty do you provide?
All furniture comes with a 5-year warranty covering manufacturing defects and structural issues. Accessories and lighting have a 2-year warranty. Enterprise customers receive lifetime warranty coverage on all purchases.
Do you offer installation services?
Yes! We offer professional installation services in select cities. Our team will deliver, assemble, and position your furniture exactly where you want it. Installation fees vary by location and product complexity - contact us for a quote.
