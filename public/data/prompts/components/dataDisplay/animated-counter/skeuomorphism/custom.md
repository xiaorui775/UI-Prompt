# Skeuomorphism - Animated Counter

## Prompt (en-US)

### Style Characteristics
Skeuomorphic animated counters mimic real-world mechanical or digital displays, using textures, gradients, and realistic shadows to create tangible, physical-looking interfaces. Numbers may appear on LED displays, mechanical flip boards, or odometer-style rollers with convincing 3D depth and lighting effects. Perfect for vintage-inspired or playful applications.

### Color Scheme
- **Display Background**: Dark (#1a1a1a, #2d2d2d) or LED green/red
- **Number (LED style)**: Bright green (#00ff00), amber (#ffaa00), or red (#ff0000) with glow
- **Number (Mechanical)**: Dark text on light background with realistic shadows
- **Container**: Metallic gradients, wood textures, or glass-like surfaces
- **Glow/Reflection**: Semi-transparent highlights and shadows for depth

### Design Details
- **Borders**: Inset borders to simulate depth, 2-4px with gradients
- **Border Radius**: 8-12px with realistic beveled edges
- **Shadows**:
  - Outer: 0 4px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.4) for container
  - Inner: inset 0 2px 4px rgba(0,0,0,0.3) for display recess
  - Text: 0 0 8-12px currentColor for LED glow effect
- **Spacing**: Generous padding 20-32px to simulate physical device housing
- **Typography**:
  - LED: 3-5rem/700 monospace, letter-spacing: 0.1em, text-shadow glow
  - Mechanical: 2.5-4rem/700 with realistic emboss or engrave effects
- **Animation**: Mechanical flip animation (rotateX), LED flicker on change, odometer roll
- **Texture**: Subtle noise, scan lines, or grain for authenticity
- **Reflection**: Linear gradient overlay for glass-like surface

## 提示詞 (zh-CN)

### 風格特點
擬物化動畫計數器模仿真實世界的機械或數字顯示器，使用質感、漸變和逼真陰影創造有形、物理外觀的界面。數字可能出現在 LED 顯示屏、機械翻板或里程表式滾輪上，具有令人信服的 3D 深度和光照效果。適合復古風或趣味應用。

### 配色方案
- **顯示背景**：深色 (#1a1a1a, #2d2d2d) 或 LED 綠/紅
- **數字（LED 風格）**：亮綠 (#00ff00)、琥珀 (#ffaa00) 或紅色 (#ff0000) 帶發光
- **數字（機械式）**：淺色背景上深色文字配逼真陰影
- **容器**：金屬漸變、木紋或玻璃表面
- **發光/反射**：半透明高光和陰影增加深度

### 細節設計
- **邊框**：內嵌邊框模擬深度，2-4px 帶漸變
- **圓角**：8-12px 帶逼真斜邊
- **陰影**：
  - 外陰影：0 4px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.4) 用於容器
  - 內陰影：inset 0 2px 4px rgba(0,0,0,0.3) 用於顯示凹陷
  - 文字陰影：0 0 8-12px currentColor 創造 LED 發光效果
- **間距**：充足 padding 20-32px 模擬物理設備外殼
- **字體**：
  - LED：3-5rem/700 等寬字體，letter-spacing: 0.1em，text-shadow 發光
  - 機械：2.5-4rem/700 帶逼真浮雕或雕刻效果
- **動畫**：機械翻轉動畫（rotateX）、LED 變化時閃爍、里程表滾動
- **質感**：細微噪點、掃描線或顆粒增加真實感
- **反射**：線性漸變疊加創造玻璃表面
